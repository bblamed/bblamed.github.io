About One Piece

One Piece is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. It follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his crew of pirates as they search for the ultimate treasure known as "One Piece" to become the Pirate King.

Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

The rubber-powered captain of the Straw Hat Pirates.

Roronoa Zoro

The swordsman who aims to be the world's best.

Nami

The navigator and thief with a love for money.

Sanji

The cook with powerful kicks and a chivalrous heart.
