About One Piece

One Piece is a legendary manga and anime series by Eiichiro Oda. It follows Monkey D. Luffy and his ragtag crew on a journey across the Grand Line in search of the ultimate treasure—the One Piece. With unforgettable characters, thrilling battles, and deep lore, One Piece has captured hearts worldwide.

Main Characters

Monkey D. Luffy

The rubber-powered captain with a dream to become Pirate King.

Roronoa Zoro

Master swordsman wielding three blades with unshakable loyalty.

Nami

The brilliant navigator with a sharp mind—and even sharper heels.

Sanji

A cook and a fighter who kicks as hard as he loves beautiful women.
